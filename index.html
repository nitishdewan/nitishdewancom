about

hi, i'm nitish — an ai tpm based in chicago, illinois. i work at rediminds where i focus on applying ai to real healthcare problems, building systems that make it into clinical workflows.

i grew up across india, before moving to chicago to study at the university of illinois chicago. winning third prize at uic impact day 2023 for a computer vision medtech solution was a moment that confirmed i was on the right track. before that, i spent time at capgemini india where i shipped projects across tech consulting and automation.

outside of work, i'm usually listening to podcasts and then talking about them, or playing pickleball when i can. i find a lot of meaning in meeting new people, hearing their stories, and helping people feel more enabled with tech and ai.
